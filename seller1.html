How Selling on Campusmart Works

1️⃣ Vendor Registration

To become a Campusmart vendor, you pay a one-time ₦5,000 registration fee. This gives you a 3-month vendor subscription and access to sell on Campusmart.

2️⃣ Product Listing Fee

Each product you want to add costs ₦200 per product. This helps us manage quality listings and platform maintenance.

3️⃣ Transaction Fees

Any applicable transaction fee will be added to the product price, so vendors don’t have to pay separately.

4️⃣ Vendor Support & Updates

After registration, detailed information about Campusmart, order handling, delivery process, and future updates will be sent directly to your email address.
