About CampusMart Online Marketplace

Connecting students — buy, sell and discover goods & services across your campus.

Who we are

CampusMart Online Marketplace is a student-centred platform designed to help students buy and sell goods and services within their campus community. From textbooks and fashion to tutoring and event tickets, CampusMart makes it easy for students to connect, trade, and support each other.

Our mission

Our mission is to empower students with a safe, affordable, and simple marketplace where campus talent and products can find a ready audience. We aim to reduce waste, lower costs, and strengthen campus communities by promoting peer-to-peer commerce.

What we offer

  • Student seller storefronts and product listings
  • Secure checkout and payment initialization
  • Delivery coordination inside campus (hall/room drop-offs)
  • Service listings (tutoring, design, photography)

Safety & Trust

CampusMart encourages safe trading practices. While we provide tools to facilitate transactions, buyers and sellers should communicate clearly, inspect goods where possible, and report suspicious activity to our support team at info.campusmart@gmail.com.

Get involved

If you'd like to partner with CampusMart, list a service, or give feedback, email us at info.campusmart@gmail.com. We're always looking for student ambassadors and campus partners.
